Edwin Mitchell

Edward Mitchell Bannister was a Black Canadian-American Tonalist painter. He was born on November 2, 1828, St. Andrews, Canada. Like other Tonalists, his style and predominantly pastoral subject matter were drawn from his admiration for Millet and the French Barbizon School.
